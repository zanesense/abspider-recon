# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Bug bounty hunters, security engineers, penetration testers, and auditors performing authorized reconnaissance and vulnerability checks.

## Product Purpose

Provide a trustworthy browser dashboard and CLI for running, monitoring, and reviewing bounded security scans with clear evidence and reports.

## Brand Personality

Professional, direct, and trustworthy. The interface should feel fast and operational without overstating certainty.

## Anti-references

Avoid theatrical “hacker” styling, decorative complexity that obscures scan state, misleading success language, and any framing that normalizes unauthorized testing.

## Design Principles

- Keep the operator oriented during long-running work.
- Make status, evidence, and next actions explicit.
- Reuse familiar product controls and the existing design vocabulary.
- Treat authorization, privacy, and uncertainty as first-class information.
- Prefer dense clarity over decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-operable controls, semantic status announcements, visible focus states, color-independent state labels, and reduced-motion-safe transitions.
